Brandon Shewmake Music

Brandon Shewmake is a composer for visual media where real or fantastical environments are a focal point. He uses a combination of instrumental and electronic sounds to compose pieces that evoke the motifs of these environments.

Brandon's personal music library continues to consist of film and video game soundtracks, which inspire his endeavors day after day. He adventured into the creational sphere of music in middle school and high school through arranging professional composers' pieces for piano, and he was honored to win second place in the Guild Wars 2008 Wintersday Art Contest for his arrangement of Jeremy Soule's idyllic, wintry pieces.
