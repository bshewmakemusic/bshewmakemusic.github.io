About

I am a composer for visual media, inspired by musical associations with real and fantastical environments.

I use a domain-driven collection of orchestral and electronic sounds to compose pieces.

I first adventured into music-making during secondary education, arranging video game and film composers' pieces for piano.

I would be glad to compose music for your video game, film, or TV series.
